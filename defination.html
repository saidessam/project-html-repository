Artificial intellegence :

(AI) is wide-ranging branch of computer science concerned with building smart machines capable of performing tasks
that typically require human intelligence. AI is an interdisciplinary science with multiple approaches
, but advancements in machine learning and deep learning are creating a paradigm shift in virtually every sector of the tech industry..
